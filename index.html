Frequently Asked Questions

How can I make my app successfull?

The success of application purely depends on targeted market and marketing to achieve users. Along with great product, it is very important to have a good marketing plan and budget to gain traction.

How do I select items to purchase?

As you browse through the ODILA App, click on the "add to shopping cart" button which items that you wish to purchase.

How do I clear my shopping cart?

To remove items from your shopping cart click on Cart icon, check the delete box for each item that you would like to remove from your shopping cart and then click on update

How would you take care of data security?

We use secured authentication token system along with data storage with encryption for sensitive data.

How much time it will take to deliver a product?

We are connecting local retailers so the delivery will be very fast than other E-commerce Site.We are planning to deliver the product to the customer within few hours only.

Can I return a product to ODILA?

Yes,If terms and conditions of replacement or refund will be there than as per guideline you will get replacement or refund.

What will be the Payment Mode?

At first we we will start with payment mode to cash after sometime customers will get online payment option including wallet.

How many type of services ODILA will provide?

All type of services which will use by people in his daily life.ODILA targeted to provide a single platform where people can take advantage of any type of product and services.
